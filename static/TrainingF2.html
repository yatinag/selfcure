Nerdy Talk

There is a release of endorphins higher than the usual level when you get addicted to food. Soon however, due to our body's feedback mechanism, it misinterprets the normal endorphin level and starts demanding for the hormone. So, if for one day you decide to eat in excess, you body will keep demanding for more food even the next time. You can either cure by fighting through it or finding an alternate source for the release of the hormone, that can be easily controlled.

Suggested Alternatives

We recommend any one of the following options which you like, as alternatives for your food addiction:

  • Reading Books
  • Music
  • Regular Exercise
  • Painting
  • Dancing

Finding An Alternative

We understand how tough, straight away leaving addictions can be. Those cravings are simply irresistible; fortunately for us, that is not the right way.

Finding an alternative helps you to transfer the energy you spend on eating food onto something else. It not only decreases the time you spend on conetrating on food, but slowly as you realise the changes in your life due to this small decision, you will eventually end up getting cured.
