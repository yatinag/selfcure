Nerdy Talk

While the working of the following methods may depend form person to person but they do temporarily work:

  • Cognitive behavioral therapy to re-train the thought process from thinking about video games into thinking about more productive thoughts such as playing outside, calling a friend or fulfilling boredom in any other way.
  • Trust building methods to help those who are socially inept to rebuild trust in others, so that they can feel comfortable while building new relationships
  • Wilderness retreats to keep users away from technology. These retreats can be highly effective because they completely eliminate all instances of technology which could lead to relapse.

Rewarding Yourself

Researches show, that rewarding yourself to a bar of chocolate or a lunch can help you by making your mind understand, that you can attain pleasure not just by playing games. It will also build self-confidence in you and make you proud of your progress towards the path of recovery.

Your mind will start associating endorphine and dopamine release to the reward that you gave instead of on gaming. This will help you to sideline your cravings towards gaming in addition to having the added fun.
