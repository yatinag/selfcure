Shopping

You must always remember to plan the reason for which you go to shop. You must clearly list the items of which you are in need, when you do so. In case there are none, and you are just going for the sake of it, then you must try to avoid it, for it holds no good. Segregating need from your greed is the most important step towards curing your shopping addiction. Also when in need, you must only purchase the items which are there on your list, and not even look at the other sections.

Understand why you shop compulsively, and elimate the source at its origin. Most shoppaholics are born out of boredom or emotional stress. Simply finding some other productive activity(like reading books or excercising) can also help you overcome your addiction.

In case you are extremely stubborn and still can't control yourself, then call upon a trusted family member, and make them in charge of always preventing you from carrying money or credit cards to shopping complexes, until and unless you show them a valid need or reason to shop

Most importantly, remember to reward yourself after any bit of success that you have on your path to recovery, after all you deserve it!!
