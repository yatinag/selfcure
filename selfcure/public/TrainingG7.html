Nerdy Talk

While it may seem far fetched, but their have been deaths related to excessively playing video games. For the most part, these deaths have been caused by exhaustion from playing for an excessive amount of time. Deaths have been reported in the United States, South Korea, China, Vietnam, and Brazil.

Building A healthy Social Environment

Your thoughts and actions are influences by the people around you. Researches show that most gaming addicts were either socially isolated, had family issues or had a bad friend circle. The addiction simply acts as an escape from the reality that the person does not like to live in.

If you fall in this category, you must understand that escaping real life challenges is never the right solution, you must always face the challenges thrown at you. You can get out of your current social group and start afresh. A cleaner environment would not let you play games, as you will realise there are so many more important things in life that you can do. And also you will have the addition of having someone to accompany you on the right path, which would make it a lot easier and more fun.
