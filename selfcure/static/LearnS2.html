Symptoms Of Substance Abuse Addictions

  • Chronic dishonesty and temper tantrums
  • Nausea, vomiting or excessive sweating.
  • Red, watery eyes; pupils larger or smaller than usual
  • Slowed or staggering walk; poor physical coordination
  • Change in overall attitude/personality
  • General lack of motivation, energy, self-esteem
  • Irregular heartbeat and a pale face
  • Secretive or suspicious behavior
