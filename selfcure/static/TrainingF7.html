Nerdy Talk

The Yale Food Addiction Scale is the first measure designed specifically to assess signs of addictive-like eating behavior. The YFAS allows for a more systematic examination of the hypothesis that ultra-processed foods (e.g., French fries, milkshakes) might trigger an addictive process for certain people. You can take the test yourself and determine on what level you are addicted to food.

Beating Boredom

Most food addictions that aren't caused due to emotional stress are a result of boredom. When one can't find anything to do, he/she starts eating whatever they can get their hands on. You must beat this boredom by doing any sort of constructive work.

You must never have snacks between meals, for they will disrupt any diets that you may have made after the previous lessons. The meals that you have must be complete, and follow the plans that you create.

Some apps and service that may make your path to recovery much more fun and interesting: Stickk, Fitocracy and DietBet.
